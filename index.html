the memories

Things that were normal.
Now basically extinct.

✈

Airport gate greetings

You walked all the way to the gate to meet someone coming off the plane. No security. No distance. Just showing up for people.

📷

Blurry disposable photos

You waited a full week to see that selfie. Then it was blurry, or someone's eyes were closed. And you kept it anyway.

🗺

MapQuest directions

Print it out, pray you don't miss a turn. No recalculating. No second chances. Just you, a piece of paper, and pure hope.

🎬

Blockbuster Friday

Browsing for a movie by holding the box was better than anything on Netflix. A random one from across the store could change your life.

📞

Answering the phone blind

You picked up without knowing who was calling. Every call was a small adventure. And you talked to their parents first.

📺

Catch it live or miss it

Your show aired at 8pm. Be there or don't see it. The stakes made every episode feel like an event.

👽

See-through Gameboy

The purple translucent shell. The way you could see the circuit board. That hit different in a way nothing designed today can explain.

🚫

Life was undocumented

You did dumb stuff and only your friends knew. Nothing lived online forever. You were allowed to just exist.

💸

Long-distance calls

The next town over cost extra. You saved phone calls for things that really mattered because they literally had a price.
