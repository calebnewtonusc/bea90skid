# bea90skid

You were born too late to be a 90s kid. This is the fix.

Be a 90s Kid is a freemium challenge platform for Gen Z teenagers and young adults who want to actually live like it's 1994, not just wear the aesthetic. Daily challenges, weekly rituals, and monthly immersions built around the habits that defined life before the internet owned everything.

## The idea

The 90s vibe is everywhere. TikTok, fashion, music. But wanting the aesthetic is not the same as having the structure to live it. bea90skid gives Gen Z that structure.

Free tier gets you started. Pro takes you all the way in.

## Challenges (examples)

- Develop a full disposable camera roll. Wait two weeks to see the photos.
- Leave a real voicemail instead of texting.
- Write a letter by hand and mail it.
- Watch your show at its air time. If you miss it, you miss it.
- Use printed directions for one trip. No GPS.

## Model

Freemium. Free tier: 3 challenges per week. Pro ($4.99/month): unlimited challenges, full curriculum, monthly immersion programs, physical artifact kit mailed quarterly.

## Target

Teenagers and young adults born in the 2000s. Old enough to romanticize the 90s. Young enough to need the structure to actually live it.
